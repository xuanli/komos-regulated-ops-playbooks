Insurance operations

Verify payer eligibility and return structured coverage evidence.

A Komos task can operate payer portals, search by member and service date, capture coverage details, and route mismatches or MFA blocks to human review.

Back to overview

Workflow Pattern

  • Receive member, payer, service date, and provider reference fields from the eligibility queue.
  • Open the payer portal with the correct credential and tenant context.
  • Capture coverage status, plan name, copay, deductible, and prior authorization flags.
  • Return evidence URLs and normalized exception reasons.
  • Escalate conflicting member data, portal outage, and MFA cases.
