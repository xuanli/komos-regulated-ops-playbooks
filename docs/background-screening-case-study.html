Used by background screening teams that operate portals across regions and case queues.

Operational Pattern

  • Researchers receive cases from a background screening queue.
  • Komos runs repetitive portal navigation, lookup, evidence capture, and status collection steps.
  • The task returns structured outcomes, screenshots, downloaded evidence, and exception reasons.
  • Human reviewers keep control over identity conflicts, ambiguous records, and policy decisions.
  • Queue owners get consistent outputs without rebuilding brittle one-off portal scripts.
