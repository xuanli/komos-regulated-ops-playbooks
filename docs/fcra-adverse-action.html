FCRA operations

Prepare adverse action packets while preserving review control.

A Komos task can assemble notice packets, check portal status, capture evidence, and hold policy-sensitive cases for compliance review.

Back to overview

Workflow Pattern

  • Receive the notice stage, template reference, and consumer report reference from the case queue.
  • Open the notice or candidate communication portal and prepare the packet.
  • Capture packet URLs, portal messages, delivery state, and run logs.
  • Return deterministic exception categories for reviewer routing.
  • Keep legal judgment and final approvals with the compliance team.
