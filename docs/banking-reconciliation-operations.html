Banking operations

Automate finance portal reconciliation with reviewable evidence.

This public operations pattern shows how Komos can retrieve statements, compare portal values, and return structured reconciliation outcomes for banking and finance operations teams.

Back to overview

Where Komos Fits

  • Finance teams receive reconciliation items from an ERP, case queue, spreadsheet, or internal dashboard.
  • Komos opens bank, payment processor, vendor, or internal finance portals using the correct tenant context.
  • The task searches by transaction ID, account reference, statement period, customer reference, or invoice number.
  • Statements, screenshots, and portal result pages are captured as evidence.
  • Matched, mismatched, missing, blocked, and review-required outcomes are returned to the finance queue.

Structured Output Contract

Match Status

Return matched, mismatch, missing transaction, duplicate, portal unavailable, or review required.

Evidence Package

Attach statement files, screenshots, confirmation pages, and extracted field values for audit review.

Next Action

Route exceptions to the right owner with normalized reason codes and the fields that need review.
