CRA operations

Run background screening portal work with auditable browser automation.

A Komos task can search court and verification portals, capture screenshots and downloaded records, and write structured outcomes back to a CRA case queue.

Back to overview

Workflow Pattern

  • Receive candidate and jurisdiction inputs from the background screening case system.
  • Open the relevant public records, employment verification, education verification, or court portal.
  • Search by the supplied identifiers and preserve portal messages, timestamps, and screenshots.
  • Return status, record summary, evidence URL, exception reason, and next action.
  • Route identity conflicts, payment issues, and ambiguous records to human review.
