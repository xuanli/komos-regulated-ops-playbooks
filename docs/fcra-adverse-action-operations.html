FCRA operations

Automate adverse action operations while preserving compliance review.

This public operations pattern shows how Komos can prepare notice packets, capture portal evidence, and route policy-sensitive cases back to compliance reviewers.

Back to overview

Where Komos Fits

  • Background screening or HR teams receive queue items with notice stage, template reference, consumer report reference, and candidate contact fields.
  • Komos opens the candidate communication, adverse action, or CRA operations portal with the correct credential context.
  • The task prepares packet materials, checks delivery status, captures portal messages, and records evidence URLs.
  • Ambiguous report data, stale candidate details, missing templates, and final approval steps are routed to reviewers.
  • Compliance teams keep legal judgment and final approval outside the automation.

Structured Output Contract

Notice Stage

Return pre-adverse, waiting period, adverse, delivered, failed, blocked, or review required.

Evidence Package

Attach packet URLs, screenshots, delivery records, and run logs for audit and reviewer inspection.

Exception Reason

Classify missing report data, template mismatch, portal error, delivery failure, and reviewer handoff.
