Insurance eligibility operations

Automate payer portal eligibility checks without losing exception control.

This public operations pattern shows how Komos can run repeatable payer portal checks, capture coverage evidence, and return structured outcomes to eligibility queues.

Back to overview

Where Komos Fits

  • Eligibility teams receive a work queue with member, payer, provider, and service date fields.
  • Komos opens the relevant payer portal or clearinghouse workflow with the correct credential context.
  • The task searches for the member, confirms coverage status, and records plan, deductible, copay, and authorization indicators.
  • Evidence artifacts and normalized outputs are returned to the source queue.
  • Ambiguous member matches, payer downtime, missing authorization, and MFA blocks are routed to reviewers.

Structured Output Contract

Coverage Status

Return active, inactive, not found, portal unavailable, or review required as a normalized status.

Evidence URLs

Attach screenshots, downloaded confirmations, or portal result captures for audit and downstream review.

Exception Reason

Classify mismatches, missing data, credential problems, payer errors, and human review handoffs.
